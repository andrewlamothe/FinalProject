☆ About oracle decks

Unlike tarot’s fixed 78-card structure, oracle decks are free-form — each deck carries its own theme, artwork, and voice. Readers pull a card to set the tone of the day, find language for what they’re feeling, or sit with a question. There is no “bad” card: every message, even a shadow, is an invitation inward. Lumina’s 44 cards move through fire, water, air, earth, and spirit.
